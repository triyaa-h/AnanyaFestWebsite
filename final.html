Our Fest Story

ANANYA fest is not just an event; it's a journey of creativity, passion, and endless fun. We bring together the brightest minds and the wildest spirits to celebrate life in all its glory.

From humble beginnings to a roaring success, ANANYA fest has become the heartbeat of our college, pulsating with energy and excitement. Join us in creating unforgettable memories and forging lifelong friendships.

At ANANYA fest, we believe in pushing boundaries, breaking stereotypes, and embracing the extraordinary. Get ready to unleash your inner spark and be part of something truly extraordinary!
